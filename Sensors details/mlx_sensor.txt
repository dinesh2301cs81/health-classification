MLX90614 Infrared Temperature Sensor

Introduction:
The MLX90614 is a non-contact infrared temperature sensor used to measure body temperature without physical contact. It is widely used in healthcare systems for safe and hygienic temperature monitoring.

Working Principle:
It detects infrared radiation emitted by objects and converts it into temperature readings using built-in signal processing.

Key Features:
- Non-contact temperature measurement
- High accuracy (±0.5°C)
- I2C communication
- Suitable for medical applications

Application in Project:
Used to measure body temperature of the patient in real-time.

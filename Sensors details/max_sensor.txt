MAX30100 / MAX30102 Sensor

Introduction:
The MAX30100/MAX30102 is a pulse oximeter and heart rate sensor used to measure SpO2 (oxygen level) and heart rate.

Working Principle:
It uses red and infrared LEDs to measure blood oxygen saturation based on light absorption in blood.

Key Features:
- Measures SpO2 and heart rate
- Low power consumption
- I2C interface
- Compact and wearable

Application in Project:
Used to monitor oxygen saturation and heart rate of the patient continuously.

Raspberry Pi Camera Module

Introduction:
The Raspberry Pi Camera is used to capture images and videos for image-based analysis.

Features:
- High-resolution image capture
- Supports real-time video
- Easy integration with Raspberry Pi
- Works with OpenCV

Working in Project:
- Captures patient image
- Sends image to ML model
- Helps in disease detection

Applications:
- Face detection
- Medical image analysis
- Smart monitoring systems
